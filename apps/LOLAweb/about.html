What is LOLAweb?

LOLAweb tests for enrichment of overlap between a query region set (a bed file) and a database of region sets. It provides an interactive result explorer to visualize the highest ranked enrichments from the database. LOLAweb is a web interface to the LOLA R package.

Can I share my LOLAweb results with others?

Yes! Once you run your region sets through LOLAweb, you are redirected to specific web page unique to your data. It will look something like this:

http://dev.lolaweb.uvasomrc.io/?key=VWQN3ZC5HFD92EK

This link can be sent to other users to share your findings.

How long are LOLAweb results kept?

LOLAweb results are stored on our servers for one year, then automatically deleted.
